📌 Context

The senior leadership of AdventureWorks Cycles, a multinational bicycle manufacturer, needed a centralized tool to monitor financial and operational performance. Monthly Excel reports were tedious to update, did not allow filtering by region or product, and made it difficult to detect deviations early. An executive dashboard was required to consolidate key KPIs, enable variance analysis (YoY, MoM), and facilitate rapid decision-making.

Business questions: How is gross margin evolving by region? Which products have the highest profitability? Which countries are outperforming or underperforming?

🎯 Objective

  • Design an interactive dashboard in Tableau that consolidates financial and operational data.
  • Enable variance analysis (month-over-month, year-over-year) with segmentation by region, product, and customer.
  • Visualize trends in revenue, costs, margin, and profitability.
  • Facilitate effective communication of results to the executive team.

🛠️ Methodology

Data Engineering

  • Extracted data from SQL Server using Docker containers on macOS.
  • Built SQL views to create a flat, denormalized table with all relevant dimensions.
  • Designed a star schema model for optimal Tableau performance.

Visualization & Interaction

  • Created KPI cards, bar charts, line charts, and heatmaps.
  • Implemented dynamic filters for year, region, and product category.
  • Incorporated storytelling: titles, annotations, and color-coded insights.

📊 Interactive Dashboard

The dashboard allows executives to explore financial performance at global, regional, and product levels. It includes KPI summaries, trend analysis, and detailed breakdowns.

💡 Business Insights

🇦🇺 Australia is the #1 Market

Generates $9.06M in revenue, significantly outperforming all other countries.

🚲 Bikes Dominate Revenue

80% of total sales come from Road, Mountain, and Touring Bikes.

📈 Healthy Margins

Overall gross margin is 41.15%, indicating strong product profitability.

🇬🇧 UK Underperformance

United Kingdom generates only $3.39M, lagging behind Australia and Germany.

📌 Executive Recommendations

  • Invest in Australia: Double down on marketing and sales resources in the top-performing market.
  • Investigate UK Strategy: Analyze sales team performance, pricing, and marketing in the UK to identify improvement areas.
  • Focus on Accessories: Bundle accessories with bikes to increase average order value and profit margins.
  • Maintain Strong Inventory: Ensure high stock levels for Road and Mountain Bikes to meet demand.
Estimated impact: Implementation of these recommendations could increase overall revenue by 8-12% and improve gross margin to 43% in the next fiscal year.
